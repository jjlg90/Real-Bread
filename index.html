What's

Sourdough
Starter

Real bread is made with only 3 ingredients: flour, water and salt. We cultivate wild yeast through sourdough fermentation, the same way that bakers used to do more than 3000 years ago.

Selected grains

But yeast is not enough to make bread. Real Bread is made with the highest quality grain of wheat, rye and hops. And our water is purified and filtered, to achieve maximum flavor.
