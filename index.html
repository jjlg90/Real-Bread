Sourdough
Starter

The recipe for Real Bread contains only 3 ingredients: flour, water and salt. We cultivate wild yeast through sourdough fermentation, the same way that bakers used to do more than 3000 years ago.

Grains

Real Bread is made with the highest quality grain of wheat, rye and hops, allowing it to achieve maximum flavor.
